The Class

The Introduction to ALgebra Course is currently the hardest course that dxe-prep has yet to offer. In this 16 week class students will be required to go to the weekly class held at ___ and will be given Lecture Notes for the class after it is done. The students will also be given a problem set which it is mandatory to do. The sets are designed to be very challenging for the students and will require dilligence and patience every week to do. The estimated homework for the class is ranges from person to person and will require a dedication from usually 3-7 hours per week.

Curriculum

Our course will be going deep into essential topics about Algebra required for higher math courses and competition math. Week 1 Follow the Rules Week 2 Fractional Exponents, Radicals, and Variables Week 3 Variables and Expressions Week 4 Linear Equations Week 5 More Variables Week 6 Linear Equations with Multiple Variables Week 7 Ratio and Percent Week 8 More Ratios and Proportion Week 9 Common Errors and Challenging Problems Week 10 Graphing Lines (Part 1) Week 11 Graphing Lines (Part 2) and Introduction to Inequalities Week 12 Graphing Inequalities Week 13 Quadratic Equations (Part 1) Week 14 Special Factorizations Week 15 Simon's Favorite Factoring Trick and Complex Numbers Week 16 Quadratic Equations (Part 2)
